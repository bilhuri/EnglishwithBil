1. Passive Voice Logic

Logika 'di-'. Fokus kalimat bergeser ke OBJEK atau AKSINYA, bukan siapa pelakunya. Pelakunya (Subject) menjadi tidak penting.

The 'di-' logic. The focus shifts to the OBJECT or the ACTION, not the actor. The actor (Subject) becomes unimportant.

// Logika Fokus:
Active: S melakukan O (Fokus pada S)
Passive: O dikerjakan oleh S (Fokus pada O)

// Contoh:
Active: John eats the pizza.
Passive: The pizza is eaten by John.

2. Passive Formula (All Tenses)

Kunci Passive Voice adalah menggabungkan To Be + Verb-3 (selalu V3!).

The key to Passive Voice is combining To Be + Verb-3 (always V3!).

// Rumus Umum Passive:
Object + TO BE (sesuai tense) + V3

// Contoh Tenses:
Present: The room is cleaned.
Past: The room was cleaned.
Future: The room will be cleaned.

3. Modals (Must / Can / May)

Modals adalah kata kerja bantu yang menunjukkan sikap (kemampuan, keharusan, kemungkinan). Ingat, Modals selalu diikuti V1 Murni!

Modals are auxiliary verbs that show attitude (ability, necessity, possibility). Remember, Modals are always followed by Pure V1!

// Formula Modals:
S + Modal + Verb-1

MUST: Keharusan Mutlak (Harus). -> You must study.
CAN: Kemampuan (Bisa). -> I can code.

4. Modals (Should / Might / Could)

Modals yang menunjukkan saran, kemungkinan lemah, atau kesopanan.

Modals that show advice, weak possibility, or politeness.

SHOULD: Saran (Seharusnya). -> You should rest.
MIGHT: Kemungkinan Lemah (Mungkin). -> It might rain.
COULD: Bentuk lampau/lebih sopan dari Can. -> Could you help me?

5. Comparative (Comparison I)

Membandingkan dua objek. Logika: A lebih B daripada C.

Comparing two objects. Logic: A is more B than C.

// Kata Sifat Pendek (1 suku kata):
Logic: Add -er + than. -> Older than.

// Kata Sifat Panjang (>2 suku kata):
Logic: Use More + Adj + than. -> More important than.

6. Superlative (Comparison II)

Membandingkan tiga atau lebih objek. Logika: Paling...

Comparing three or more objects. Logic: The Most...

// Wajib ada 'the' di depan.
// Kata Sifat Pendek:
Logic: The + Add -est. -> The oldest.

// Kata Sifat Panjang:
Logic: The Most + Adj. -> The Most beautiful.

7. Prepositions of Place I (In, On, At)

Logika umum: In (di dalam area), On (di atas permukaan), At (di lokasi spesifik/titik).

General logic: In (inside an area), On (on a surface), At (at a specific location/point).

IN: Countries, Cities, Rooms. -> In Jakarta, In the office.
ON: Surfaces, Streets. -> On the table, On Sudirman Street.
AT: Specific points. -> At the corner, At the bus stop.

8. Prepositions of Place II (Over, Under, Beside)

Preposisi yang menunjukkan hubungan ruang antar benda.

Prepositions showing the spatial relationship between objects.

OVER: Di atas, tapi tidak menyentuh. -> The cloud over the hill.
UNDER: Di bawah. -> The cat under the table.
BESIDE: Di samping. -> Next to / Beside the chair.

9. Conjunctions (Basic: And, But, Or)

Kata hubung sederhana untuk menggabungkan ide yang setara.

Simple conjunctions to join equal ideas.

AND: Menambah (Adding). -> I like silver and gold.
BUT: Kontras (Contrast). -> He is smart but lazy.
OR: Pilihan (Choice). -> Coffee or tea?

10. Conjunctions (Correlative: Both...And)

Kata hubung berpasangan untuk ide yang kompleks (paling sering dipakai di TOEFL/IELTS).

Paired conjunctions for complex ideas (often used in TOEFL/IELTS).

Both...and: Keduanya. -> Both the project and the team are ready.
Not only...but also: Tidak hanya...tapi juga. -> Not only smart but also kind.
