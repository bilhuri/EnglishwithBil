1. Noun Clause Connectors

Aturan #1 TOEFL Structure: Satu kalimat hanya boleh punya SATU Verb Utama. Jika ada dua Verb, butuh 1 kata penghubung (Connector).

TOEFL Rule #1: One sentence may only have ONE Main Verb. If there are two Verbs, you need 1 Connector.

// Formula:
S1 + V1 + Connector + S2 + V2

-> I know what you said. (✅)
-> I know what you said is true. (❌ Error: Kurang Subjek untuk Verb 'is')

2. Parallel Structure

Jika ada list (A, B, dan C), bentuk tata bahasanya (gerund, noun, verb) harus SAMA. Sering muncul di Written Expression.

If there is a list (A, B, and C), the grammatical form (gerund, noun, verb) must be the SAME. Common in Written Expression.

// Formula:
Item A V-ing, Item B V-ing, and Item C V-ing

-> He likes coding, reading, and writing. (✅)
-> He likes coding, reading, and to write. (❌ Error: Tidak Paralel)

3. Advanced Inversion

Membalik Subjek dan Aux/Modal. Digunakan untuk penekanan setelah kata-kata negatif/tempat.

Inverting Subject and Aux/Modal. Used for emphasis after negative words or place phrases.

// Kata Negatif Wajib:
Never, Rarely, Only, Seldom, Not only

// Rumus:
Negative Word + Aux/Modal + Subject + Verb

-> Never have I seen this. (✅)

4. Gerund/Infinitive Pitfalls

Kata kerja apa yang harus diikuti -ing (Gerund) dan kata kerja apa yang harus diikuti To + V1 (Infinitive).

Which verbs must be followed by -ing (Gerund) and which by To + V1 (Infinitive).

// Wajib Gerund (-ing): Avoid, Finish, Mind, Enjoy, Suggest.
-> He avoids working. (✅)
-> He avoids to work. (❌)

// Wajib Infinitive (to V1): Agree, Decide, Hope, Plan, Want, Need.
-> I plan to buy gold. (✅)

5. Listening Distractors

Strategi TOEFL Listening: Jawaban benar SANGAT JARANG menggunakan kata-kata yang sama persis dengan yang Anda dengar. Hindari jawaban yang menggunakan *keyword* yang diucapkan. Cari *paraphrase*nya.

TOEFL Listening Strategy: The correct answer is RARELY the one using the exact words you hear. Avoid answers using the spoken *keywords*. Look for the *paraphrase*.

6. Listening Note-Taking

Fokus pada kata kunci: Siapa (W), Apa (W), Kapan (W), Mengapa (W). Gunakan singkatan dan simbol (+, -, >, $).

Focus on keywords: Who, What, When, Why, and symbols (+, -, >, $). Do not write full sentences.

7. Reading Skimming Strategy

Jangan baca semua teks. Baca kalimat pertama setiap paragraf untuk memahami ide utamanya. Gunakan scanning (mencari keyword spesifik) untuk pertanyaan detail.

Don't read the whole text. Read the first sentence of each paragraph for the main idea. Use scanning (looking for specific keywords) for detailed questions.

8. Context Vocabulary

Jika menemukan kata yang tidak tahu artinya, jangan panik. Baca kalimat sebelum dan sesudahnya untuk menebak. Jawaban di TOEFL seringkali adalah sinonim (persamaan kata).

If you find an unknown word, don't panic. Read the sentences before and after to guess. Answers in TOEFL are often synonyms.

9. Reference Logic (It / They)

Pronomina (It, They, She, He) selalu merujuk pada kata benda tunggal/jamak yang muncul di kalimat SEBELUMNYA. Cek apakah cocok secara jumlah (tunggal/jamak).

Pronouns (It, They, She, He) always refer to a noun (singular/plural) that appeared in the PREVIOUS sentence. Check for number agreement (singular/plural).

10. Pacing & Mindset

Atur waktu. Jangan terlalu lama di satu soal. Jika bingung, jawab secepatnya dan fokus ke soal berikutnya. Di TOEFL, kecepatan sama pentingnya dengan akurasi.

Manage your time. Don't spend too long on one question. If stuck, answer quickly and move to the next question. In TOEFL, speed is as important as accuracy.
