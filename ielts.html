1. Writing Task 1: Map Logic

Tugas 1 (Task 1): Analisa data (grafik/tabel/peta). Kuncinya adalah TIDAK MEMBERI OPINI. Fokus hanya pada perbandingan dan perubahan (naik/turun).

Task 1: Data analysis (charts/maps). The key is NO OPINIONS. Focus only on comparison and changes (rise/fall).

// Struktur Wajib:
1. Intro (Paraphrase Pertanyaan)
2. Overview (2 Tren Utama)
3. Body 1 (Detail Data)
4. Body 2 (Detail Lain)

// Wajib Gunakan:
Noun: a significant increase
Verb: increased significantly

2. Writing Task 2: 4-Paragraph Plan

Tugas 2 (Essay): Selalu gunakan struktur 4 paragraf. Jangan membuat 5 atau 6 jika waktu Anda terbatas.

Task 2 (Essay): Always use a 4-paragraph structure. Do not write 5 or 6 if your time is limited.

// Struktur Essay Wajib:
P1: Introduction (Thesis Statement)
P2: Body 1 (Idea Utama + Support)
P3: Body 2 (Idea Lawan/Lain + Support)
P4: Conclusion (Ringkasan & Penegasan Ulang)

3. Cohesion & Connectives

IELTS menilai seberapa baik ide Anda terhubung. Gunakan kata penghubung (connectives) yang beragam di awal setiap paragraf.

IELTS assesses how well your ideas connect. Use varied connective words at the beginning of each paragraph.

// Connectives (Wajib Variasi):
Adding: Moreover, Furthermore
Contrasting: However, On the other hand
Result: Consequently, As a result

4. Complex Sentence Range

Untuk Band Score 7+, Anda harus menggunakan kalimat majemuk. Gabungkan ide menggunakan klausa (though, although, which, who).

For Band 7+, you must use complex sentences. Combine ideas using clauses (though, although, which, who).

// Contoh Klausa:
-> The project which I started yesterday is finished now.

-> Although coding is hard, I enjoy it.

5. Listening Prediction Strategy

Sebelum audio dimulai, baca pertanyaan dan tebak jenis jawabannya (angka, nama tempat, kata sifat). Ini membuat Anda fokus saat mendengarkan.

Before the audio starts, read the questions and guess the type of answer (number, place name, adjective). This helps you focus when listening.

6. Listening Trap Words

Waspadai jebakan. Setelah seseorang memberi jawaban (misal: "I will go on Tuesday"), mereka sering mengatakan "but..." atau "actually...". Jawaban yang benar adalah yang muncul SETELAH kata "tapi".

Beware of traps. After someone gives an answer (e.g., "I will go on Tuesday"), they often say "but..." or "actually...". The correct answer is what comes AFTER the word "but".

7. Reading T/F/NG Distinction

Membedakan T/F/NG adalah kunci Reading. True: Informasi persis ada (paraphrase). False: Informasi bertentangan. Not Given: Informasi tidak dibahas sama sekali.

Distinguishing T/F/NG is key. True: Information is present (paraphrased). False: Information contradicts the text. Not Given: Information is not discussed at all.

8. Reading Skimming/Scanning

Gunakan Skimming (baca cepat) untuk ide utama dan Scanning (cari spesifik keyword) untuk jawaban detail. Jangan pernah membaca teks kata per kata.

Use Skimming (reading fast) for main ideas and Scanning (finding specific keywords) for detailed answers. Never read the text word-by-word.

9. Speaking Fluency and Coherence

Jangan terlalu khawatir tentang grammar sempurna, kekhawatiran itu menyebabkan Anda terlalu sering berhenti (pause). Prioritaskan kelancaran (fluency) dan keterhubungan ide (coherence) menggunakan kata sambung.

Don't worry too much about perfect grammar, that worry causes you to pause too often. Prioritize fluency and idea connection using linking words.

10. Speaking Idioms & Lexis

Untuk Band 7+, gunakan kosakata yang tidak umum (less common vocabulary) dan frasa idiomatik. Hindari kata-kata seperti "good", "bad", "happy". Ganti dengan "terrific", "detrimental", "over the moon".

For Band 7+, use less common vocabulary and idiomatic phrases. Avoid words like "good", "bad", "happy". Replace them with "terrific", "detrimental", "over the moon".
